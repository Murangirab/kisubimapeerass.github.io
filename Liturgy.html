Do you understand the word communication? (Can mean sending and receiving of information). Yes, communication is a very important aspect in our day today life. Big companies develop because of communication, schools run smoothly because of communication, families live in peace because of communication, friends keep together because of communication.
There is Joy, Happiness, Peace if we receive and send communication to our beloved ones. For instance, our Parents, Siblings, Relatives as well as friends. That feeling is so awesome. With that background, let’s take a simple look at one unique communication which we may
perhaps under look or take for granted, but it’s the greatest of all communications that we may think of. Its non-other than prayer.
Prayer is the communication between God and man. We should love prayer, we should
make prayer simple, we should make prayer enjoyable. Mother Theresa once said, “God
speaks in the silence of the heart, listening is the beginning of prayer”.
Joyce Meyer, described“prayer as simply talking to God like a friend and should be the easiest thing we can do each day”. Kahlil Gibran once said “prayer is the song of the heart that reaches the ear of God”. That interaction between God and man can yield a lot of good things. As that song goes Great things happen when God mixes with man. James 4:10 “Humble yourselves in the presence of Lord, and he will exalt you”.
Psalms 37:4 “Take delight in the Lord, and he will give you the desires of your hearts”. Jeremiah 29:12 “Then you will call me and come and pray to me, and I will listen”.
Dear friends we should always remember that we worship a living God, a caring Father, an understanding person, merciful father, a person who is not judgmental, sympathetic father, forgiving, powerful, all knowing. God is waiting on us all the time. We should run to him through prayer, because prayer has got power to change situations. In a nutshell, let us change our mindset towards prayer, lets us cherish it, value it, and
always keep in mind that we are conversing with the Holy one and the owner of our life.

Kisubi Mapeera SS
