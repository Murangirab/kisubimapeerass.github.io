Kisubi Mapeera SS

 

Before us came great women of great importance to the church and the world at large.
These were leaders who still stand now as an inspiration to other female leaders. Here I
stand taking the example of Esther and Ruth who were judges. Being a leader does not
mean to own a title, it is not swearing in nor is it being tested and examined.
Leadership comes from God the Almighty Father. He chooses the ones who were born
to be and who have always been. He chooses them as examples and everyone has their
own obligation to fulfill in their term of leadership. A round of applause to the president of
Uganda and all other presidents around the world. Though a man, he emphasizes gender
equality and to testify, the speaker of Uganda for the eleventh Parliament is right honorable
Anita Among “A LADY” She stands tall as an example and reason why I was able to stand
as the HEAD GIRLL KISUBI MAPEERA SS 2022 – 2023.
As it is the role of the Almighty God to choose, one had to be very ready for any challenge
that comes during their term. Be it political, social or economic.
It is not easy to lead without ruling but no leader can rule without leading. One has to
pray for God’s guidance as God chooses leaders before anyone else. As a matter of fact,
leadership is serving because a lot is given in. As a “student leader”, one offers their time
for class but this robs noting off one’s education because, it is God who chooses leaders
and so guides them. Leaders should always create extra time for their own lives and other
personal responsibilities. It takes nothing for one to wake up at right and read their books
or even stay and extend more than other students.
To my fellow student leaders, please top in everything. A leader leads with example
and without the right example; other students (the ones you lead) will take you for granted.
Please bring them close so that they can open up to you about whatever they think or even
intend to do. BUT PLEASE, DO NOT let them feel they are above you.
On my side, being a student leader has given me the best of leadership. A duo leader
shows everyone positivity. For me, there is nothing like I only support either teachers or
students. An surprisingly, no one can even notice the side am not supporting at a particular
time. Let’s be leaders and never rulers. I thank the Almighty God for His knowledge, wisdom
and protection and I pray that He may guide us all to become the best in our sectors that
God chose us for. For my school, I pray that the Almighty makes it live for generations and
develop to infinity.
