Academic Programs

A'Level Academic Program

The Advanced Level Academic Program Follows the national curriculum teaching for two years from S5 -S6 of rigorous academics within either science or Arts subjects. At the beginning of this level, Students must choose three principal subjects and one subsidiary subject. These selections form subject combinations which are examined at the national level at the end of two years. 

Academic Programs

O'Level Academic Program

The Ordinary Level Academic Program Follows the national curriculum teaching for four years from S1 to S4 of rigorous academics within mathematics, science subjects, English, and Social Science Subjects. Students must also choose from various skilling subjects that are also examined on a national level at the end of four years.
